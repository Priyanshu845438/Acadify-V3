Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our social media marketing services

The best platforms depend on your target audience and business goals. We typically recommend starting with 2-3 platforms where your audience is most active. For B2B: LinkedIn and Twitter/X. For B2C: Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok. We conduct audience research to determine the optimal platform mix for your specific business.

Posting frequency varies by platform and audience. Generally, we recommend: Instagram (1-2 times daily), Facebook (1-2 times daily), LinkedIn (1-5 times per week), Twitter/X (3-5 times daily), TikTok (1-3 times daily). Quality and consistency matter more than frequency. We develop a posting schedule based on your audience's online behavior and engagement patterns.

Initial engagement improvements can be seen within 2-4 weeks, but significant growth typically takes 3-6 months of consistent effort. Paid advertising can drive immediate results, while organic growth requires patience and consistency. We provide monthly reports showing progress in followers, engagement, reach, and conversions to track improvement over time.

We handle all content creation including graphics, videos, and copy. However, we also incorporate your existing content, brand materials, and behind-the-scenes content you provide. User-generated content and authentic business moments often perform best, so we'll guide you on what type of content to share with us for maximum impact.

We track metrics aligned with your business goals: engagement rates, reach, followers growth, website traffic from social, lead generation, and sales conversions. We provide detailed monthly reports with insights and recommendations. Key performance indicators vary by business type and objectives, which we establish during our initial strategy phase.

Yes! We provide crisis management and reputation monitoring as part of our services. We respond to negative comments professionally and promptly, escalate serious issues to you when needed, and help maintain a positive brand image. We also monitor brand mentions across platforms and address concerns proactively.

Absolutely! We identify and collaborate with influencers who align with your brand values and target audience. We handle outreach, negotiate partnerships, manage campaigns, and track performance. We work with micro-influencers, macro-influencers, and industry experts based on your budget and goals.

Our packages typically include strategy development, content creation, posting schedule management, community engagement, monthly analytics reports, and ongoing optimization. Specific deliverables vary by package level, but all include dedicated account management and transparent reporting. We also offer add-ons like paid advertising management and influencer partnerships.
