1. The Master AI UI Generation Prompt (For the New Hero Section)

This is the new, definitive prompt for the first section of every service and industry page.

"UI design for a premium, full-width 'Hero' section for a service/industry page. The section must be flush with the bottom of the main navigation bar, with no gap.

Background & Style:

The background is a solid, deep, professional navy blue (#0d3169).

Overlaid on the background is a very subtle, semi-transparent pattern of a digital grid or blueprint lines, giving the section a high-tech, engineered feel.

At the bottom of the section, there is a clean, curved shape divider that provides a smooth, elegant transition to the white/light-gray content that follows.

Layout (Two-Column):

Left Column (Content):

All text is white for maximum contrast and readability.

A large, bold headline using Poppins font, speaking directly to the benefits of [Service/Industry Name].

A supporting paragraph in a slightly muted white Roboto font.

A primary call-to-action button, styled as a solid white pill with blue text.

Right Column (Visual):

A high-quality visual element (a clean device mockup for services, or an evocative photo for industries) that pops against the dark blue background. The visual should have a soft, outer glow to make it stand out.

The overall design must be immersive, premium, and create a powerful first impression."
